Facebook Ads Management

Why Your Facebook Ads Aren't Performing

Most businesses running Facebook Ads are making the same structural mistakes. Campaigns are built on interest-stacking that limits the algorithm. Creative is running until it fails rather than being rotated proactively. The pixel is firing browser-side only with no CAPI backup, so Meta is working with half the signal it needs.

The result: high CPM, inconsistent ROAS, campaigns that work for a month then drop off. The problem isn't Facebook Ads as a platform — it's how the campaigns are built and managed.

FRX Media has managed over $36M in Facebook ad spend. We've seen every failure mode, built the fixes, and systematised them into a campaign framework that delivers 10–13× ROAS consistently across eCommerce categories.

What Our Facebook Ads Management Covers

  • Creative strategy — hook testing, angle development, fatigue monitoring
  • Audience build — broad, LAL, retargeting by intent level

  • Advantage+ campaigns — AI-optimised delivery at scale

Our Facebook Ads Services

A Complete Facebook Advertising System

Every FRX Media client gets a full-stack Facebook Ads system — not a menu of individual services bolted together.

Cold Traffic Campaigns

Broad audience cold traffic campaigns built to exit the learning phase fast. Creative does the targeting — we write hooks that filter out tyre-kickers and pull in buyers.

Retargeting Campaigns

Intent-segmented retargeting — homepage visitors, product page visitors, add-to-cart, and checkout abandoners each get a different message. Not one ad for everyone.

Lookalike Audiences

Lookalike audiences seeded from your highest-value customers — not just all purchasers. We build LTV-weighted seed audiences that find buyers, not browsers.

Pixel & CAPI Setup

Server-side Conversions API alongside browser pixel. Event deduplication configured. Signal quality maintained above 8.0. Meta gets the data it needs to optimise.

Advantage+ Shopping

Advantage+ Shopping Campaigns managed for eCommerce brands with proven creative. AI-optimised delivery combined with human creative strategy — the best of both.

Performance Reporting

Weekly reports covering every metric that matters — ROAS, CPM, CTR, CPA, frequency, and creative performance. Monthly calls to review and set scaling direction.
