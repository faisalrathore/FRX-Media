How much does Meta Ads lead generation cost for roofing companies?+

Cost per lead varies by market and storm activity. As a benchmark, a comparable home-services Meta lead-gen campaign we managed generated 136 leads at an average cost of $4.16 per lead.

Do you run Google Ads and Microsoft Ads as well as Meta Ads for roofing?+

Yes. FRX Media runs Meta Ads, Google Ads (including Local Services Ads), and Microsoft (Bing) Ads for roofing companies, recommending the channel mix based on where your best leads convert.

Can you activate campaigns quickly after a storm?+

Yes. We pre-build storm-response campaign structures so they can be activated within hours of a major weather event, which is when roofing demand spikes hardest.

How fast can I see results from a roofing ads campaign?+

Storm-response campaigns can generate leads within days of activation. Full re-roof campaigns have a longer consideration cycle, typically 2-4 weeks to see stabilized cost per lead.

How do you handle the trust factor for a high-ticket purchase like roofing?+

We build creative around licensing, insurance, and warranty messaging, and use review and case-study proof points, since trust signals matter more for roofing than lower-ticket home services.
