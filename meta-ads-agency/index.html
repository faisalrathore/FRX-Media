What Is a Meta Ads Agency and Why Does It Matter?

A Meta Ads agency manages your Facebook and Instagram advertising end-to-end — campaign architecture, creative strategy, audience targeting, pixel and CAPI setup, budget allocation, and ongoing optimisation. The goal is one thing: turning your ad spend into measurable, predictable revenue.

Most businesses running Meta Ads in-house or with a generalist agency leave significant ROAS on the table. Campaign structure is wrong. Creative isn't being tested. The pixel is mis-firing. Audiences are too narrow or too broad. Budget is scaling before the algorithm has enough data to exit the learning phase.

FRX Media fixes all of this. We've managed over $36M in Meta ad spend across 9 industries and built a campaign system that consistently delivers 10–13× ROAS for eCommerce and D2C brands.

Audience Targeting

Campaign Scaling

Systematic budget scaling using cost controls, duplicate-and-scale methodology, and Advantage+ budget optimisation. We scale when the data says scale — not before. No guesswork, no blown budgets.

Performance Reporting

Weekly reports covering ROAS, CPM, CTR, CPA, frequency, and creative performance. Monthly strategy reviews with forward-looking scaling recommendations. Full transparency, no vanity metrics.
