Why Your Instagram Ads Aren't Converting

Instagram is the highest-intent visual platform for eCommerce — but most brands are running it wrong. Creative is stale, targeting is over-narrowed, and the pixel isn't feeding Meta's algorithm the data it needs to find buyers efficiently.

The result is high CPM, low CTR, and a feed full of ads that look like ads. Instagram's algorithm rewards native-looking content that earns genuine engagement. When your creative fails that test, you pay more for every impression and get fewer conversions.

FRX Media has managed Instagram advertising campaigns across 9 eCommerce categories. We build creative systems that look native, test relentlessly, and consistently deliver 10–13× ROAS for D2C brands.

How much does Instagram Ads management cost?+

What ROAS can I expect from Instagram Ads?+

FRX Media clients average 10–13× ROAS on Instagram advertising. Our peak result is 13.44× ROAS for a health supplement brand. Results depend on your product, offer strength, creative quality, and existing data.

What's the difference between Instagram Ads and Facebook Ads?+

Instagram and Facebook Ads are managed through the same Meta Ads Manager platform. Instagram placements (feed, Reels, Stories, Explore) are selected at the ad set level. FRX Media manages both simultaneously, optimising budget allocation between placements based on performance data.

Do you manage Instagram Ads internationally?+

Yes. We manage Instagram advertising campaigns for clients in the US, UK, UAE, Australia, Canada, Singapore, and India. All management is remote with full weekly reporting.

How important is creative for Instagram Ads?+

Creative is the single biggest performance lever on Instagram. Meta's own data shows creative quality drives over 56% of campaign performance. On Instagram specifically — a visual-first platform — creative that looks native to the feed consistently outperforms polished brand content. We treat creative strategy as core to every campaign, not an afterthought.
