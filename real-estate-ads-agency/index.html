Real Estate Ads Management

Why Real Estate Ad Campaigns Underperform

Real estate advertising fails for two reasons: lead volume or lead quality. High-volume campaigns that generate hundreds of leads but 90% are unqualified time-wasters. Or tightly targeted campaigns that generate too few leads to justify the management fee.

The fix is a structured lead qualification system built into the ad creative and lead form itself. The right hook pre-qualifies intent. The right lead form questions filter budget range, timeline, and location before the lead even reaches your team. You pay for fewer leads and close more of them.

FRX Media has delivered 200+ leads at $4 CPL for lead generation clients. We apply the same structured approach to real estate — building campaigns that generate volume at low CPL while filtering for qualified buyers from the first touchpoint.

Questions About Real Estate Ads

Do you specialise in real estate advertising?+

FRX Media is a performance marketing agency with deep expertise in lead generation campaigns. We apply our proven lead generation framework — which has delivered 200+ leads at $4 CPL — to real estate projects. Our focus is always qualified leads at predictable CPL, which is exactly what real estate sales teams need.

What platforms do you use for real estate advertising?+

We primarily use Meta Ads (Facebook and Instagram) for volume lead generation and Google Search Ads for high-intent buyers actively searching for properties like yours. The right mix depends on your project type, price point, and target buyer.

How do you ensure lead quality?+

Lead quality is built into the campaign from the start — through the hook creative that pre-qualifies intent, the lead form questions that filter budget and timeline, and the offer framing that attracts serious buyers. We also track which leads convert to site visits and sales, so we can optimise toward quality — not just volume.

What's a realistic CPL for real estate campaigns?+

CPL varies significantly by market, price point, and project type. Luxury developments in competitive markets will have higher CPL than affordable housing in emerging markets. We'll give you a realistic CPL estimate for your specific project during the free audit call.

Do you work with real estate developers internationally?+

Yes. FRX Media manages real estate advertising campaigns for developers and agents in the US, UK, UAE, Australia, Canada, Singapore, and India. We have specific experience with UAE and Indian real estate markets where Meta Ads generates high lead volumes at competitive CPL.
