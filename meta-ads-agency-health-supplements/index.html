Health & Supplement Meta Ads

Why Supplement Brands Struggle With Meta Ads

Health and supplement advertising on Meta comes with unique challenges — strict policies around health claims, high competition in popular categories like protein and vitamins, and a buyer who needs to trust the brand before purchasing. Most supplement brands lose budget to policy violations, weak creative hooks, or campaigns that can't exit the learning phase.

The brands winning in health and supplements on Meta are leading with transformation stories, social proof, and benefit-specific hooks rather than clinical claims. They're running broad audiences and letting creative do the targeting. And they have server-side CAPI configured so Meta's algorithm gets the full purchase signal it needs to optimise.

FRX Media's peak ROAS of 13.44× was achieved for a US health supplement brand. We understand Meta's health advertising policies, the creative angles that convert in this category, and the campaign structures that scale supplement brands profitably.

Our Health & Supplement Ad Services

Meta Ads Built For Supplement Brands

Every health and supplement campaign we build is structured around Meta's advertising policies, the specific psychology of health buyers, and the LTV economics of subscription supplement businesses.

Policy-Compliant Creative

Health and supplement ads must navigate Meta's strict health claims policies. We write compliant copy that drives results — benefit-led, transformation-focused, and built to pass review without sacrificing conversion rate.

Testimonial & Social Proof Ads

Customer testimonials and results-based social proof are the highest-converting formats for supplement cold traffic. We structure, brief, and test these systematically across multiple buyer personas.

Subscription Campaigns

Supplement subscription models require specific campaign architecture — acquisition campaigns focused on first purchase, and retention campaigns maximising LTV. We build both and optimise toward subscriber value, not just CPP.

Bundle & Upsell Ads

Post-purchase retargeting campaigns promoting bundles, complementary products, and subscription upgrades. The most profitable traffic is your existing buyers — we build campaigns to maximise their LTV.

CAPI & Pixel Setup

Server-side CAPI integration is critical for supplement brands — iOS privacy changes reduced browser pixel signal quality significantly. We configure full CAPI setup so Meta gets the purchase data it needs to optimise efficiently.

Performance Reporting

Weekly reports covering ROAS, CPP, subscription rate, LTV by cohort, and creative performance. Supplement business economics require metrics beyond just ROAS — we track what matters.

FAQ

Questions About Health & Supplement Meta Ads

What ROAS can supplement brands expect from Meta Ads?+

FRX Media's peak ROAS is 13.44× achieved for a US health supplement brand. Realistic expectations depend on your product price point, margins, offer structure, and creative quality. Most supplement brands we work with see meaningful improvement within 60 days.

How do you navigate Meta's health advertising policies for supplements?+

We write benefit-led, transformation-focused copy that avoids medical claims, disease treatment language, and before-and-after imagery that violates Meta's health policies. Our creative briefs are built around what Meta allows — and our copy converts within those guidelines.

Do supplement subscription models work well with Meta Ads?+

Yes — subscription supplement brands are ideal for Meta Ads because LTV is high. We build acquisition campaigns optimised for first purchase and separate retention campaigns maximising subscription continuation. The economics of supplement subscriptions allow higher initial CPP when LTV justifies it.

What's the minimum ad spend for supplement brands?+

We work with supplement brands from $1,000/month in ad spend upward. For subscription models where LTV is high, $5,000+ per month gives the algorithm enough purchase events to optimise subscription acquisition efficiently.

Do you manage Meta Ads for supplement brands internationally?+

Yes. We manage supplement Meta Ads for brands in the US, UK, UAE, Australia, and Canada. The US market is our primary focus for supplement brands — it has the highest volume and purchase intent for health and wellness products.
