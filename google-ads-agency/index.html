Why Your Google Ads Are Wasting Budget

Most Google Ads accounts are structurally broken. Broad match keywords eating budget on irrelevant searches. Smart campaigns with zero visibility into what's actually working. Shopping feeds with missing attributes suppressing impression share. Performance Max campaigns running without asset testing or placement exclusions.

The result is a high cost per click, low conversion rate, and an account that looks busy but isn't profitable. Google Ads rewards structured campaigns with clean data, tight keyword control, and continuous bid strategy testing — not set-and-forget automation.

FRX Media manages Google Ads alongside Meta Ads for clients who want full-funnel paid acquisition. We bring the same data-driven, performance-first approach to Google that has delivered 13.44× ROAS on Meta.

How We Work

Our Google Ads Process

01

Account Audit

We audit your existing Google Ads account — keyword structure, match types, negative lists, bid strategies, conversion tracking, and Quality Scores. We find where budget is leaking before we fix anything.

02

Restructure & Launch

Campaigns rebuilt on a clean structure — tight ad groups, exact match core keywords, comprehensive negative lists, and conversion tracking verified before spend increases.

Weekly review of search term reports, Quality Scores, bid adjustments, and conversion data. We add negatives, pause poor performers, and shift budget to what's working.

Once target CPA or ROAS is stable, we scale budgets systematically. New keyword expansion, additional campaign types, and competitor targeting added as data builds.
