Fashion & Apparel Meta Ads

Why Fashion Brands Struggle With Meta Ads

Fashion is one of the most visually competitive categories on Instagram and Facebook. Every brand has good-looking content. The brands that win on Meta aren't just running prettier ads — they're running smarter campaigns with better audience structures, faster creative testing cycles, and catalogue setups that show the right product to the right person at the right time.

The biggest mistakes fashion brands make on Meta: over-relying on interest targeting instead of broad audiences, running the same creative for 60+ days without testing new angles, and not setting up dynamic product ads to recover abandoned carts and product viewers.

FRX Media has scaled fashion and apparel brands across multiple markets. We understand the seasonal dynamics, the catalogue management requirements, and the creative cadence that keeps fashion campaigns performing consistently.

FAQ

Questions About Fashion Meta Ads

How often should fashion brands refresh their Meta Ads creative?+

Fashion audiences are highly visual and experience creative fatigue faster than most categories. We recommend refreshing creative every 2–3 weeks for active campaigns. We monitor frequency and CTR decline weekly and brief new creative before fatigue impacts ROAS.

Do you manage Instagram Shopping for fashion brands?+

Yes. Instagram Shopping catalogue setup, product feed optimisation, and Shopping ads management are included. We also manage collection ads and carousel formats that are particularly effective for fashion discovery on Instagram.

What ROAS can fashion brands expect from Meta Ads?+

Fashion ROAS depends heavily on your product price point, margins, and creative quality. We've achieved $18.20 CPP for 1,844 purchases over one year for a jewellery brand. For fashion, realistic ROAS targets of 3–8× are common depending on price point — higher AOV products naturally support higher ROAS.

How do you handle seasonal fashion campaigns?+

We plan campaign structures around your collection calendar in advance — new drops get awareness campaigns 1–2 weeks before launch, sale periods get urgency-led retargeting, and post-season periods focus on list building for the next collection.

Do you manage fashion ads internationally?+

Yes. We manage fashion Meta Ads for brands in the US, UK, UAE, Australia, and Canada. The UAE market is particularly strong for fashion and luxury apparel — high purchase intent and strong Instagram engagement.
