Why Beauty Brands Struggle With Meta Ads

Beauty and skincare is one of the most competitive categories on Meta. Every brand is running aspirational creative, targeting the same interest audiences, and wondering why CPMs are high and ROAS is inconsistent. The brands winning on Meta in beauty aren't spending more — they're running smarter campaigns.

The key difference is creative strategy. Beauty is a visual category where the product needs to be demonstrated, not just displayed. UGC-style reviews, before-and-after content, ingredient storytelling, and influencer-style hooks consistently outperform polished brand photography for cold traffic acquisition.

FRX Media has scaled beauty and skincare brands on Meta across the US, UK, UAE, and Australian markets. We understand the creative formats that convert in this category and the audience structures that keep CPMs competitive.

FAQ

Questions About Beauty & Skincare Meta Ads

What creative formats work best for beauty brands on Meta?+

UGC-style review videos, before-and-after content, and ingredient/benefit storytelling consistently outperform polished brand photography for cold traffic in beauty. On Instagram specifically, Reels ads with a strong hook in the first 2 seconds deliver the lowest CPM and highest CTR in this category.

How do you handle Meta's policies around before-and-after beauty ads?+

Meta has specific policies around before-and-after imagery and health claims. We structure beauty creative to be compliant — focusing on the experience and transformation rather than medical or clinical claims. Our copywriters understand these guidelines and brief creative accordingly.

What ROAS can beauty brands expect from Meta Ads?+

FRX Media has achieved 13.44× peak ROAS in the beauty category. Realistic expectations for a new brand depend on product price point, margins, offer strength, and creative quality. Most beauty brands we work with see meaningful ROAS improvement within 60 days of restructuring.

Do you work with beauty brands internationally?+

Yes. We run beauty and skincare Meta Ads campaigns for brands in the US, UK, UAE, Australia, and Canada. Beauty performs particularly well in the UAE market where Instagram engagement and purchase intent are both high.

How much should a beauty brand spend on Meta Ads?+

We work with beauty brands from $500/month in ad spend upward. For meaningful scaling with creative testing, $3,000–$5,000/month gives the algorithm enough data to exit the learning phase and optimise efficiently.
