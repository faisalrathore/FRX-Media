Convention Rules
  • UPPERCASE at every level
  • Pipe ( | ) between components
  • Hyphens within a component
  • No spaces or special characters
  • Date is optional, typed by you — DD/MM/YY, DD/MM, or MM/YY
  • Date field appears at the end of every level
  • Never rename after launch — version it
  • "None" or a blank field omits that component entirely
  • "Custom" lets you type your own value for any field
  • Agency prefix is optional — leave blank to hide it everywhere
